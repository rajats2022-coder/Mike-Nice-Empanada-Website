What Makes Them Different

Mike Nice leans into thick, handcrafted empanadas with bold Puerto Rican, Hispanic, American, and Soul Food fusion flavors. The brand is fun, local, and built around the question: What’s in your Pocket?

Signature Flavors

Birria, Pollo Loco, El Boriqua, Philly Cheesesteak, Buffalo Chicken, Jerk Chicken, Smoked Mac & Cheese, Vegan Chorizo, and dessert empanadas.

Where to Find the Truck

The truck serves the Triangle with stops around Knightdale, Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Clayton, Chapel Hill, and surrounding communities. Follow Instagram for the latest weekly schedule.
