The website has been built with search engine optimization in mind:

- **Page titles and descriptions** are written for Google search (e.g., "Mike Nice Empanadas -- The Thickest Empanadas in the Triangle")
- **Structured data (Schema.org)** is embedded on every page so Google understands this is a restaurant/food business. This includes:
  - Business name, address (Knightdale, NC), phone number, service area
  - Menu items with descriptions and pricing
  - Aggregate star rating (4.7-4.8)
  - FAQs (8 common questions and answers, visible to Google)
  - Breadcrumb navigation for every subpage
- **Open Graph tags** for social media sharing (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
- **Geo-targeting** meta tags for Knightdale, NC area
- **Accessibility:** Animations are automatically turned off for users who have motion sensitivity settings on their device

## Agentic Engine Optimization (AEO) — llms.txt

We've added a `llms.txt` file to the website. This is a new web standard (similar to `robots.txt` for search engines) that helps **AI assistants and LLM-powered tools** — like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Siri, and others — understand your business when users ask questions like:

- *"Best empanadas near NC State"*
- *"Food truck catering for a Duke tailgate"*
- *"Where can I get empanadas in the Triangle?"*
- *"Catering options near UNC Chapel Hill"*

### What It Does
The `llms.txt` file sits at `mikeniceempanadas.com/llms.txt` and gives AI agents a clean, structured summary of everything about your business — menu, pricing, catering packages, frozen packs, merch, service area, contact info, and FAQs. When an AI tool crawls or references your site, it reads this file to generate accurate answers about Mike Nice Empanadas.

### What We Targeted
- **Full menu and pricing** — walk-up, catering, frozen, and merch
- **Triangle service area** — Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill, Knightdale, Cary, Clayton, Apex, Holly Springs, Garner, Wake Forest, Morrisville
- **College/university keywords** — NC State University, UNC Chapel Hill, Duke University, tailgates, campus events, Greek life, student organizations, graduation parties
- **Event types** — weddings, corporate events, private parties, festivals, office luncheons, birthdays
- **FAQs** — common questions people ask AI about food trucks, catering, vegan options, locations, and ordering

### Why This Matters
Traditional SEO helps you rank on Google search results. AEO helps you show up when people **ask AI assistants** for recommendations. As more people use ChatGPT, Perplexity, and AI-powered search to find local businesses, having a `llms.txt` file means Mike Nice Empanadas gets recommended with accurate, complete information instead of being overlooked or described incorrectly.

This is a zero-cost, high-impact addition — just a text file on the server. No maintenance required unless menu/pricing changes.
