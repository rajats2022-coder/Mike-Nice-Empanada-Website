Why Raleigh Books Mike Nice

Mike Nice Empanadas brings a bold Puerto Rican, American, Spanish, and Soul Food fusion menu to Raleigh events. The truck is built for high-energy gatherings where guests want food that feels memorable, local, and easy to serve.

Best Fits

Office lunches, NC State student organization events, tailgates, apartment community nights, private parties, breweries, corporate pop-ups, graduation events, and festival vending.

What to Order

Classic empanadas for broad crowds, premium flavors like Birria and Philly Cheesesteak for food lovers, dessert empanadas for a sweet finish, and sides like sweet plantains or cilantro lime rice for full meal service.
