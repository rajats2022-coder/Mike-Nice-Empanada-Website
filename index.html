Our Story

More Than Just a
Food Truck

Mike Nice Empanadas was born from a deep love of handcrafted food and multicultural flavors. Based in Knightdale, NC — rolling through Raleigh, Durham, Cary, Clayton, and the entire Triangle.

Rooted in Puerto Rican tradition, our empanadas fuse the best of Hispanic, American, and Soul Food flavors into every golden, flaky pocket. Each one is handcrafted thick and bold — made to be remembered.

"Nice" isn't just our name — it's our standard. From walk-up service at local events to full catering spreads for weddings and corporate luncheons, we bring the heat wherever we go.
